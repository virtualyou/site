Intimate Collaboration

Very private; Closely personal; Yet distanced

We learned from the Harvard Business Review that people over 50 make half up of the consumer spending in the United States. Yet to many businesses, they might as well be invisible. We however, estimate potential customers currently at roughly 184 million people. That's also assuming only people over 50 need our service. AARP has found that 11% of family caregivers live an hour or more away from aging parents or loved-ones. This figure seems low but this is still around 20 million aging people in need of long-distance caregiving. The question for us (VirtualYou) is, "how many adults are in need of personal online assistance of any age?".

Maintaining your affairs online is an excellent solution for the computer savvy. Our children and our elderly may be social media experts but they lack the knowledge to use the computer for life's work. Also, there's the challenge to get the important stuff entered, safe in the cloud, and break the procrastination to actually do this work. Once your information is online, private to you, and somewhere you can use it, you need a central location to manage this data. You most certainly will want a few others to have access to it as well. All this hinges on your ability to get it all uploaded. Well, maybe your team's ability to work together and load it.

Help is on it's way!

Owner

You are the owner. This is your domain. And everyone needs a little help now and then. VirtualYou is here to help. First and foremost, our focus is on data gathering and making that task simple and easy. Our goal is to gather data by interview and and make use of the power of artificial intelligence (AI).

Agent

As an agent for the owner, you have read and write privileges to the owner's domain. You also have a great responsibility on your shoulders however, VirtualYou is here to ensure that your assistance is private to your relationship with the owner.

Monitor

Maybe an owner feels strongly about not letting someone else edit their data. That's where the monitor can have a relationship with the owner at VirtualYou and be allowed read-only access to the owner's domain.
