Inspiration

Deep within the mind of David L Whitehurst, lay an idea to allow Mom to use her computer for something other than a social-media platform.

Since it's inception in 2019, VirtualYou has been a dream of mine because the service it has to offer goes beyond the simple web application. The need is global. I've worked with people from different countries and many of them leave family in their country of origin to seek opportunity in the United States of America. My current goal is to start the application in the US, and when the time is right, host clustered services around the world. Our user's can try things with other english-speaking men and women outside of the US, but the goal will be hosted zones.
